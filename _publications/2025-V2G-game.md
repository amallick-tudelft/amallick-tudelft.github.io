This paper presents a user-centric Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G) framework utilizing a game-theoretic, horizon-splitting strategy to dynamically arbitrate between financial revenue and battery health preservation based on explicit user preference signals. The approach integrates an enhanced semi-empirical degradation model and is validated for robustness and sensitivity under parameter uncertainty against standard multi-objective optimization benchmarks.
